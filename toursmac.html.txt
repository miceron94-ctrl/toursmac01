<section id="china">
  <h2>Tour in China</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Airlines:</strong> Air China, China Eastern, China Southern.</li>
    <li><strong>Services:</strong> Guided tours, high-speed train, Great Wall visits, 4 and 5 star hotels.</li>
    <li><strong>Important dates:</strong> Chinese New Year (January–February), Mid-Autumn Festival (September).</li>
    <li><strong>Typical food:</strong> Peking duck, dim sum, noodles, fried rice.</li>
    <li><strong>Customs:</strong> Respect for elders, use of chopsticks, punctuality.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section id="france">
  <h2>Tour in France</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Airlines:</strong> Air France, Lufthansa, Iberia.</li>
    <li><strong>Services:</strong> Cultural tours, museums, river cruises.</li>
    <li><strong>Important dates:</strong> Bastille Day (July 14).</li>
    <li><strong>Typical food:</strong> Croissant, cheese, baguette, escargots.</li>
    <li><strong>Customs:</strong> Formal greetings and love for art and food.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section id="japan">
  <h2>Tour in Japan</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Airlines:</strong> Japan Airlines, ANA.</li>
    <li><strong>Services:</strong> Bullet trains, traditional hotels, bilingual guides.</li>
    <li><strong>Important dates:</strong> Cherry blossom season (March–April).</li>
    <li><strong>Typical food:</strong> Sushi, ramen, tempura.</li>
    <li><strong>Customs:</strong> Bowing and removing shoes indoors.</li>
  </ul>
</section>
